Goal of the game to rearrange checkers from yours corner (of the house) to the corner of the opponent before he built his in your corner. Moves are made one by one. Movement is possible for horizontals and verticals only towards the corner of the opponent. In one move, it moves no more than one checkers. Checker can either move one space or jump over one or more checkers.

The game ends when one player has built all their checkers in the house of the enemy. All checkers must be put out of his home at least 3 * 'number of pieces' moves - otherwise it will be counted a loss.
